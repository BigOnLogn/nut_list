Nut List is a feed filter I threw together for the Music Nuts Facebook group. You may search by date, by user, or both.

Some examples of search:

Dates

12/23/12 or 12/23/2012 translates to all posts from 12/23/2012

Real English time frame

2 days ago or yesterday or last week translates to all posts from {given time frame}

Until date

You can search for all posts until a given date by adding one of the keywords "until", "to", or "up to". until 12/23/12 or to 12/23/12 or up to 12/23/12 All the above give the same result.

Between two dates

You can search for posts made between two dates by using the between/and keywords. between 1/1/12 and 12/23/12

By Members

You can search for posts made by specific members by selecting them from the "by who?" text box. Just start typing and there will be a list of members to choose from

Combination Search

Any information entered into either text box will be incorporated into the search, i.e.: 12/23/12 and Kate E Anderson will find all posts made by Kate since 12/23/2012.

Enjoy!
